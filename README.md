**Hotel Menu & Sales Optimization**

* Project Overview: 
The Hotel Menu & Sales Optimization project focuses on analyzing hotel food sales data to improve menu performance and increase revenue through data-driven insights.
By studying customer purchasing behavior and item-wise sales trends, the project helps identify profitable dishes and areas requiring improvement.

* Objective: 
Analyze hotel food sales data
Identify top-selling and low-performing menu items
Understand customer ordering patterns and revenue trends
Provide actionable recommendations for menu and pricing optimization

* Dataset: 
The dataset contains historical hotel sales records, including:
Menu item names
Quantity sold
Sales revenue
Category or type of items
Order-level details
(Data is used only for analytical and learning purposes.)

* Methodology: 
Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
Handled missing values and inconsistencies
Prepared structured data for analysis
Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
Item-wise and category-wise sales analysis
Revenue contribution and frequency analysis
Trend and pattern identification

* Visualization: 
Graphical representation of sales trends
Comparison of high-performing vs low-performing items
Insights & Recommendations
Menu optimization suggestions
Pricing and inventory planning support

* Tools & Technologies
Python
Pandas, NumPy
Matplotlib / Seaborn
  
* Key Insights: 
Identified best-selling and low-demand menu items
Analyzed customer preferences and purchase behavior
Highlighted revenue-driving dishes
Supported data-driven menu optimization decisions
